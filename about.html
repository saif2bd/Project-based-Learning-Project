Our Legacy & Future

Uniting the global community of Albukhary International University graduates under one banner of Adab and Altruism.

Who We Are

The AIU Alumni Association is an independent organization committed to fostering a lifelong connection between the university and its graduates. We believe that graduation is not the end, but the beginning of a lifelong journey of giving back to society.

Our Mission

To build a robust network of social entrepreneurs and leaders who embody the 5As (Adab, Akhlaq, Amanah, Altruism, Abundance) and contribute significantly to global peace and prosperity.

Our History

2010

Albukhary International University was established by the Albukhary Foundation.

2014

First Convocation Ceremony held at the majestic Dewan Canselor.

2018

Re-establishment of the university with a renewed focus on Social Business.

2025

Launch of the Digital Alumni Portal connecting over 50 countries.
